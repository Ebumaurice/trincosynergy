Renovation & Remodelling

Transforming Existing Spaces With Precision

Renovation is more than upgrading finishes — it is a careful balance between respecting existing structures and introducing new functionality, efficiency, and architectural clarity.

At Trinco Synergy, we approach renovation projects with strategic planning, technical accuracy, and minimal disruption, ensuring every intervention adds long-term value.

Types of Renovation Services

Residential Renovation

Home upgrades, interior remodelling, and functional reconfiguration.

Commercial Renovation

Office, retail, and workspace renovations with operational efficiency.

Hospitality & Retail

Hotels, restaurants, and public-facing spaces designed for impact.

Institutional Upgrades

Schools, clinics, and civic facilities renovated to modern standards.

What We Renovate

  • Interior remodelling & space reconfiguration
  • Structural alterations (where applicable)
  • Façade and exterior upgrades
  • Electrical, plumbing & MEP upgrades
  • Finishes, fixtures & material replacement
  • Accessibility & compliance improvements
