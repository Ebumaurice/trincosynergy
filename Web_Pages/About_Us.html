Company Story

Our History

Trinco Synergy Nig Ltd is a leading building and civil engineering contracting firm in Nigeria. Established in 1932 by Ilorah Tochukwu, it is nearing a century of unmatched expertise, making it one of the most historic and respected construction companies in the country.

With a team of highly skilled professionals and modern technologies, the company has built a legacy founded on excellence, innovation and commitment. Over the decades, Trinco Synergy has expanded into Facility Management services and advanced aluminium & curtain walling systems, strengthening its presence in Nigeria’s construction landscape.

Leadership

Our Chief Executive Officer

Ilorah Tochukwu serves as the Chief Executive Officer of Trinco Synergy Nigeria Limited, providing strategic direction and leadership to one of Nigeria’s most enduring construction firms. With decades of industry knowledge, he has upheld the company’s reputation for precision, reliability, and uncompromising quality.

Under his leadership, Trinco Synergy has evolved with modern construction technologies, expanded into facility management and advanced façade systems, and consistently delivered projects that meet international standards. His vision continues to drive sustainable growth, innovation, and long-term value for clients and communities.
